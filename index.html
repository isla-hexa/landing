ABOUT

Strategically located in the Pacific shipping lanes, Isla Hexa has served as a vital maritime waypoint since 1847. Our naturally formed deepwater harbor, protected by unique volcanic reef formations, accommodates vessels of all classifications.

Today, we balance our rich maritime heritage with sustainable tourism and cutting-edge port operations, welcoming over 2,000 vessels annually.

TOURISM

Experience the raw beauty of an active volcanic island. From our famous black sand beaches to the summit of Mount Hexa, adventure awaits at every turn. Our six distinct coastal regions offer unique experiences for every traveler.

Popular activities include volcano tours, deep-sea diving at Hexagon Reef, and exploring our historic port district's maritime museum.

PORT OF HEXA

The Port of Hexa operates one of the Pacific's most advanced vessel tracking systems, serving commercial shipping, research expeditions, and specialized cargo operations. Our six primary berths handle everything from container ships to scientific vessels.
